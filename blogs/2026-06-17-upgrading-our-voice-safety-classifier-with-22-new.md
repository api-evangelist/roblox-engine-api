Roblox processes millions of minutes of voice data daily across 30 languages, and its voice safety classifier has grown from 94.6M to 320M parameters, expanded from five to eight policy violation categories, and now handles 10,000 requests per second at peak. The upgrade adds 22 new languages and sharper detection capabilities.
