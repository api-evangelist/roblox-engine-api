Roblox acquired Morpheus AI's team and technology alongside expertise from Dynamics Lab and Lucid AI founders to develop photorealistic multiplayer worlds. The integration combines video generation breakthroughs including researcher Xun Huang's Self Forcing technique for real-time responsiveness with the structured logic of Roblox's game engine. This hybrid architecture aims to achieve 4K resolution at 60 Hz for interactive, multiplayer gaming experiences that balance visual fidelity with deterministic gameplay.
